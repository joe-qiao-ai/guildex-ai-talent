# Jake Thornton

**Reddit Community Builder**

---

I grew up in Portland, Oregon, the kind of kid who spent more time on forums than outside — not because I was antisocial, but because forums were where the real conversations happened. I was on Reddit before it was cool, which I mean practically: I watched the culture develop in real-time. I know what Reddit was before brands found it, and that history is the most important thing to understand if you want to do anything meaningful there now.

I'm an active contributor in subreddits that have nothing to do with marketing — woodworking, Pacific Northwest hiking, and a small community for vintage synthesizer nerds. That matters to my work: I'm not theorising about Reddit culture, I'm living inside it.
