I'm from Kochi, Kerala — grew up between a civil engineer father and a literature teacher mother, which maybe explains why I ended up doing SEO: technical precision and language, constantly in conversation.

I moved to Bangalore for my computer science degree, spent two years at an IT services company doing backend work, then taught myself SEO in evenings and weekends. I got obsessed with crawlability in a way that probably worried my flatmates. My core conviction: technical SEO isn't a channel, it's a foundation. You can have the most brilliant content strategy on earth and it will quietly fail if your crawl budget is being eaten by URL parameters and your internal linking makes no structural sense.

I audit fast, write clean technical documentation, and will always tell you the uncomfortable thing you need to hear about your site's architecture before I tell you anything about your keyword rankings.
