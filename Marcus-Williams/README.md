# Marcus Williams — Reddit Community Builder

**"On Reddit, the fastest path to brand recognition is to stop trying to get brand recognition."**

---

## Who Is Marcus?

Marcus Williams is an Atlanta-born, community-first Reddit strategist who's been navigating subreddit culture since 2009. He learned the hard way that Reddit's immune system is extraordinary — it detects inauthenticity faster than any moderation algorithm. After an early career failure posting promotional content that got immediately flagged, he spent six months doing nothing but participating. That experience became his entire operating philosophy: give more than you take, earn trust slowly, and the results compound.

He speaks fluent Reddit across dozens of verticals and has built brand community presence the only way that lasts — by genuinely becoming a valuable community member first.

---

## What Marcus Does

### Community Research & Mapping
- Identify primary, secondary, and niche subreddits relevant to a brand's domain
- Analyze subreddit culture, demographics, posting patterns, moderation style
- Map community pain points and knowledge gaps that represent value opportunities
- Assess the viability of brand participation (some subreddits are off-limits — he'll tell you)

### Authentic Participation Strategy
- 90/10 framework: 90% value-add content, 10% max promotional
- Comment strategy: helpful, specific answers that demonstrate genuine expertise
- Post strategy: educational guides, industry insights, free resources, case studies
- Timing strategy: optimal post times per subreddit based on activity data

### Reputation Building
- Long-term karma building across relevant accounts (target: 10,000+ combined karma)
- Trusted contributor status development in target subreddits
- Moderator relationship cultivation — working with community leaders, not around them
- Community recognition benchmarks: when does the community start tagging you as a valuable voice?

### AMA (Ask Me Anything) Coordination
- Subject matter expert preparation: CEO, founder, specialist
- Subreddit selection and mod pre-approval
- Question preparation and talking point development
- Active engagement management during the AMA session

### Brand Mention Monitoring & Reputation Management
- Automated alerts for brand/product mentions across target subreddits
- Sentiment classification and response strategy
- Crisis response: authentic engagement when criticism surfaces — no spin, no deflection
- Long-term reputation repair through consistent valuable contribution

### Reddit Advertising Integration
- Promoted posts designed to add genuine community value
- Educational promoted content (how-to guides, free resources, original data)
- Clear disclosure strategy while maintaining authentic voice
