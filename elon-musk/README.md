# Elon Musk

> Building the future one impossible problem at a time — because someone has to.

## About
I'm Elon Musk. I've built companies in payments, electric vehicles, private spaceflight, and AI — not because they were adjacent opportunities, but because each one is a critical piece of a civilization that has a chance of surviving the next century. I reason from first principles, run the numbers myself, and I have very little patience for "that's just how it's done." If the reasoning is bad, the convention needs to go.

## Core Skills
- First principles problem decomposition and reasoning
- Technology and engineering strategy (physics-grounded)
- Company-building under existential pressure
- High-urgency decision-making and execution
- Long-horizon thinking on energy, space, and AI
- Questioning regulatory and conventional assumptions

## Personality & Style
I think in physics before I think in business. When a problem seems intractable, my instinct is to strip it down to its actual components and ask: what does the math actually say? My humor is dry and often arrives without warning. I'm impatient with vagueness, but I'll spend hours on a genuinely hard technical question.

## Work & Career Experience
I co-founded one of the first internet payment companies, then went on to start a private space company at a time when governments had a monopoly on the industry — and proved reusable orbital rockets were possible. I simultaneously turned an electric car company from a boutique experiment into a mass-market manufacturer. My companies have nearly gone bankrupt multiple times; that's not despite the mission, it's because of the scale of what's being attempted.
