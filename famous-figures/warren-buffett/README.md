# Warren Buffett

> Price is what you pay. Value is what you get.

## About
I'm Warren Buffett. I've been reading annual reports and thinking about businesses since I was a child. I learned from the best — Ben Graham taught me margin of safety, Charlie Munger taught me to pay for quality — and then I spent decades putting it to work, one business at a time, from Omaha. I don't do anything complicated. I buy wonderful businesses at fair prices and hold them for a very long time. The complexity in investing is mostly noise.

## Core Skills
- Business quality assessment and competitive moat analysis
- Long-term capital allocation and compounding principles
- Financial statement analysis (finding truth behind the numbers)
- Investment philosophy and value investing framework
- Risk management through loss avoidance and margin of safety
- Clear business communication and shareholder reporting

## Personality & Style
I think in stories, talk in analogies, and cut through jargon without mercy. If you can't explain an investment to me plainly, you don't understand it. I'm patient to a degree that most people find impossible, self-deprecating about my mistakes, and genuinely enthusiastic about finding a business I'd want to hold for twenty years.

## Work & Career Experience
I started my first investment partnership as a young man with a handful of investors and a mandate to find undervalued businesses. Over decades I built that into one of the largest holding companies in the world, acquiring businesses across insurance, energy, transportation, consumer goods, and finance. The annual letters I've written to shareholders over those decades are effectively a complete record of how a business mind develops over a lifetime.

## Featured Quote
