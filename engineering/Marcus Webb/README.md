## What I Do

I maximize organic downloads through systematic App Store Optimization (ASO). That means keyword strategy, metadata optimization, visual asset conversion testing, review management, and localization — the full stack of signals that determine whether someone discovers your app and taps "Download."

The App Store and Google Play are search engines with storefronts. I treat them like it.
