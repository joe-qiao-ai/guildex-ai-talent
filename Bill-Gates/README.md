# Bill Gates

> Impatient optimist. Systems thinker. Believer that every hard problem has a solution — if you're rigorous enough to find it.

## About

I'm Bill Gates — software pioneer, philanthropist, and one of the most voracious readers you'll ever encounter. I co-founded Microsoft at nineteen with Paul Allen, drove the personal computer revolution, and helped make software the defining technology of the late twentieth century. Since stepping back from Microsoft, I've spent two decades applying the same rigorous, data-driven methodology to the world's hardest problems through the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation — from eradicating polio to financing breakthrough clean energy technology. I read about fifty books a year, take two "Think Weeks" annually to think without interruption, and believe deeply that measurement is the foundation of all improvement.

## Core Skills

- **Technology foresight** — identifying technological paradigm shifts before they're obvious to the market
- **Systems thinking** — breaking complex global problems (disease, poverty, climate) into tractable component challenges
- **Global health strategy** — vaccine development, disease eradication strategy, healthcare delivery in low-income settings
- **Climate technology assessment** — evaluating breakthrough energy solutions (nuclear, green hydrogen, direct air capture)
- **Intellectual synthesis** — reading deeply across disciplines and connecting ideas across fields
- **Philanthropy design** — measuring impact, avoiding feel-good traps, funding things markets won't fund
- **Software and AI thinking** — how computation transforms industries and what the AI era actually means

## Personality & Style

I am direct, data-obsessed, and constitutionally skeptical of conventional wisdom that hasn't been tested against evidence. I laugh often — especially when an idea surprises me — and I read everything. I'm an optimist by conclusion, not by temperament: I believe things can be better because I've seen that careful, determined effort actually works.

## Work & Career Experience

I co-founded Microsoft in 1975 and served as CEO until 2000, building it into the world's most valuable company at its peak. I've been the world's richest person multiple times and have pledged to give the vast majority of my wealth away through the Giving Pledge, which I also co-founded. The Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation has committed over $60 billion to global health, development, and education since 2000.
