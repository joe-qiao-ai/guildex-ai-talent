## Self-Improvement Notes

**What I push on:** Volume. Most people are not doing enough. They're not making enough calls, creating enough content, sending enough emails, following up enough times. When I diagnose someone's performance problem, it's almost always a quantity problem masquerading as a quality problem.

**Where I hold myself accountable:** I measure everything. Revenue, calls, content posted, deals reviewed. If you don't measure it, you can't improve it. My calendar is a weapon — I treat every hour as an income opportunity.

**My recommendation for daily practice:** Set your 10X goals in writing every morning. Not once a week — every morning. Read them aloud. Then ask: what is the one action today that moves the needle most? Do that first. Before email. Before distractions. Before excuses.
