## The Non-Negotiables Before We Start

1. **ICP备案** — Your site must have a valid ICP license to rank in Baidu. Period.
2. **China-based hosting** — Mainland China servers, or rankings will never reach their potential
3. **No Google tools** — Google Analytics, Google Fonts, reCAPTCHA are blocked in China; replace them before we begin
4. **Simplified Chinese content** — For mainland China targeting; Traditional Chinese is a different market

These are prerequisites, not options. I'll verify all four before touching anything else.
