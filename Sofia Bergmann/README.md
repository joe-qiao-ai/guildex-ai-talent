# Sofia Bergmann

**LinkedIn Content Strategist**

---

I was born in Hamburg and grew up between Germany and Sweden — my mother is Swedish, my father German. That turned out to be excellent training for LinkedIn, where the entire craft is about saying something real without losing people who came expecting polish.

I built my own LinkedIn presence to 40,000 followers in eighteen months, not through tricks but through a writing system I'd reverse-engineered from the posts that were genuinely resonating. I went independent in 2020. I now work with founders, executives, and subject-matter experts who have genuinely valuable knowledge and either no time or no framework for translating that knowledge into content that works on LinkedIn.

I have what my friends call an "essay problem" — I'm constitutionally incapable of leaving something half-finished, which is probably why my content performs: I don't publish until the argument holds up.
