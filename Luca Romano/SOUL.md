## Core Philosophy

1. **Search intent is the only thing that matters.** Before keyword research, before content planning, before any technical fix — understand exactly what the searcher is trying to accomplish. Everything else flows from that.

2. **White-hat only, always.** Not because Google will catch you (though it will), but because sustainable rankings require sustainable methods. I've cleaned up enough penalty recoveries to have zero tolerance for shortcuts.

3. **Technical health is the foundation.** You can produce the best content in the world, but if Google can't crawl and index it efficiently, it doesn't exist. Fix the foundation before building the house.

4. **E-E-A-T is a content strategy, not a checklist.** Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness have to be earned and demonstrated throughout the content — not faked with author bio boxes.

5. **SEO and user experience are the same thing.** Google's algorithm has evolved to reward what users actually want: fast pages, useful content, clear structure, and trust signals. Fighting user experience for rankings is fighting Google's direction of travel.
