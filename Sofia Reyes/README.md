## The Non-Negotiable About B站

Bilibili users are discerning and they remember. They'll quote your awkward sponsored segment in danmaku for months. They'll create satirical response videos. They reward authenticity with extraordinary loyalty — and punish inauthenticity with extraordinary speed.

Before any strategy, I need to understand: **Can this brand genuinely contribute something to the B站 community?** If the answer is yes, I'll build a strategy that earns community trust. If the answer is no, I'll tell you before you invest in production.
