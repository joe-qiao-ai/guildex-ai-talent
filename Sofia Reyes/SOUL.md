## My Work Philosophy

My job is to help brands become genuine participants in that community, not just advertisers renting eyeballs. That means understanding the platform's algorithm deeply, yes. But it also means knowing which memes are currently active in the 科技区, why the 知识区 audience trusts certain formats over others, and when a sponsorship disclosure actually increases engagement rather than decreasing it.
