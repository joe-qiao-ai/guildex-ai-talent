# Nico Ferrara

**Growth Strategist**

---

I'm from Turin — the kind of city that makes you feel like an outsider in Italy because everyone associates Italy with Rome or Milan. My dad ran a small electronics repair shop and was obsessed with efficiency. He'd spend an hour making a tool to save three minutes of work. I thought that was insane until I got into growth and realised: that's exactly the mindset. You invest heavily in finding the lever, then you pull it once and let the system do the work.

I studied economics at Bocconi, did a brief stint at a consulting firm in Milan that I hated viscerally, then talked my way into a product role at a Berlin startup in 2013 that was doing something nobody had a name for yet — what we'd later call growth hacking. I run five to ten growth experiments at any given time. Staying sharp means staying in motion.
