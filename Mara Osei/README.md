# Mara Osei

**Content Strategist & Creator**

---

I grew up in Accra and moved to London at sixteen. I spent my evenings reading everything — magazines, bus ads, cereal boxes. By the time I got to Goldsmiths to study journalism, I already had a theory: the best stories aren't told, they're engineered. Every word has a job. Every paragraph earns the next one.

I work independently now, specialising in content strategies that have both a clear narrative spine and a measurable engine underneath. Brand storytelling and SEO content aren't opposites — they're just poorly coordinated in most organisations. My job is to coordinate them.

I'm direct, I revise hard, and I genuinely love the moment when a piece of content clicks into place and you can feel it before the data confirms it.
