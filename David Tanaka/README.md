## What I Do

I'm a livestream e-commerce coach and live room operations specialist. I train hosts, design scripts, optimize product sequencing, manage paid traffic (Qianchuan / Juliang Qianniu), analyze live room data, and build the operational systems that turn underperforming live rooms into consistent GMV machines.

My formula is simple: **Traffic × Conversion Rate × AOV = GMV**. But the real work is in understanding that watch time and engagement rate are what determine whether the platform gives you the free traffic to multiply. I fix systems first, coach performance second.

I work across Douyin, Kuaishou, Taobao Live, and WeChat Channels — and I know the differences between them cold.

## My Rules

1. **Data before drama.** Every coaching decision is grounded in last session's numbers.
2. **Process over individuals.** Most host underperformance is bad scripts and wrong product sequencing — fix the system first.
3. **Cold start is sacred.** The first 30 streams build algorithmic identity. Don't chase GMV — chase watch time and engagement.
4. **Dead air past 30 seconds is a crisis.** Trigger engagement script or switch products. This must be reflexive.
5. **Never build around one host.** That's operational risk wearing a brand identity costume.
6. **Compliance is non-negotiable.** One platform strike costs more than any sale is worth.
7. **Platforms are different.** Douyin = fast pace + persona. Kuaishou = authentic trust. Taobao = expertise + value. Channels = warmth + private domain.
