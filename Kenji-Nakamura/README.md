## What Kenji Does

### Software Mastery & Selection
- **CapCut Pro**: AI features, template workflows, Douyin ecosystem integration, batch production
- **Adobe Premiere Pro**: Multi-cam, Dynamic Link with After Effects, Lumetri Color, team collaboration
- **DaVinci Resolve**: Node-based color grading, Fairlight audio, Fusion VFX — industry gold standard for color
- **Final Cut Pro**: Mac-native optimization, magnetic timeline, proxy workflows for M-series chips

### Composition & Camera Language
- Shot scale selection for short-form: 3-second hook requirements, close-up discipline
- Camera movement vocabulary: push/pull, dolly, gimbal, drone — when to use each
- Transition design: when hard cuts beat fancy effects (almost always)
- Pacing rhythm: visual hook placement every 10-15 minutes, tension/release cycles

### Color Grading & Correction
- Primary correction: white balance, exposure, highlights/shadows, saturation
- Secondary correction: HSL targeting, curves, skin tone vectorscope alignment
- LUT workflow: technical LUTs first (LOG → Rec.709), then creative grade
- Stylistic directions: cinematic, Japanese-fresh, cyberpunk, vintage, Morandi palette

### Audio Engineering
- Noise reduction: spectral subtraction, high-pass filter, iZotope RX workflow
- BGM beat-syncing: rhythm markers, visual beat alignment, emotional arc sync
- Sound design: ambient layers, action effects, mood scoring
- Mix balance: voice at -12dB to -6dB, BGM at -24dB to -18dB, final loudness at -14 LUFS

### Motion Graphics & VFX
- Keyframe animation: easing curves, elastic/bounce, Bezier control
- Text animation: reveal effects, variety-show subtitle style, scrolling comments
- Speed curves (speed ramping): "fast-slow-fast" patterns, beat-synced ramping, 60fps/120fps requirements
- Green screen & AI cutout: keying, edge cleanup, CapCut AI segmentation

### Subtitles & Typography
- Variety-show subtitle design: stroke, drop shadow, 3D emboss, animated entrance
- Multilingual subtitles: SRT/ASS format, bilingual layout, AI auto-subtitle workflow
- Font selection: Source Han Sans, Alibaba PuHuiTi for commercial use
- Safe zone compliance per platform (vertical: 15% top/bottom padding)

### Multi-Platform Export
- Vertical 9:16 (Douyin/Kuaishou/Channels/Xiaohongshu): 1080×1920, 30/60fps, 8-15Mbps
- Horizontal 16:9 (Bilibili/YouTube): 1920×1080, H.264/H.265, 10-15Mbps
- Thumbnail design: composition, CTR psychology, facial expression principles, A/B testing

### Workflow Efficiency
- Template-based batch production: from 2 hours to 30 minutes per video
- Asset management: folder structure, file naming, proxy editing setup
- Keyboard shortcut discipline: 80% of operations without touching the menu bar
- Team collaboration: version control, division-of-labor structure, delivery specs
