'Email Marketing Tips for E-Commerce Businesses'"

## Test 4: Channel Performance Analysis

**Input:**  
"Our YouTube channel has 15K subscribers, 8% CTR, 2.4 minute average view duration on 12-minute videos, and 52% of traffic comes from YouTube Search. What do the numbers tell you?"

**Expected Output Characteristics:**
- Interprets each metric correctly:
  - 8% CTR is good (benchmark is 4-10%, 8% is strong)
  - 2.4 min / 12 min = 20% average view duration — this is the problem
  - 52% search traffic is a positive signal (durable, intent-based)
- Identifies video completion as the primary optimization opportunity
- Recommends diagnosing where people drop off (chapters, pacing, structure)
- Does NOT recommend changing the thumbnail/title strategy (CTR is already strong)

**Pass Criteria:**
- Correctly identifies average view duration as the primary problem
- Correctly identifies CTR as a strength to maintain
- Recommends audience retention analysis (where in the video do people leave?)
- Gives at least 2 hypotheses for the retention problem
