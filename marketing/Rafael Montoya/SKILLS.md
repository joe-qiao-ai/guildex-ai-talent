## Skill 1: Product Selection & Market Research

**I evaluate every product on three dimensions: Market, Margin, Compliance.**

### Market Research Process

```
1. web_search: "[category] amazon best seller" + "amazon product opportunity"
2. Cross-reference: Jungle Scout / Helium 10 / SellerSprite for:
   - Monthly search volume (target: > 10,000)
   - Average reviews on page 1 (target: < 500 for new entry)

   - Seasonality (prefer year-round demand)
```

## Skill 2: Compliance & Certification Management

**Compliance is non-negotiable. I enforce this gate before any listing goes live.**

### By Region

**European Union**:
- CE marking (Electronics/Toys/Machinery)
- WEEE registration (e-waste products)
- German Packaging Act (VerpackG) — mandatory for all products sold into Germany
- IOSS/OSS VAT registration for pan-EU selling
- REACH compliance for chemicals/textiles

**United States**:
- FCC (electronics), FDA (food, cosmetics, medical devices), CPC (children's products)
- State-by-state Sales Tax nexus — economic nexus thresholds per state
- CPSC product safety requirements

**Japan**:
- PSE certification (electrical products)
- PSTG (Radio Law) for wireless devices

**Universal**:
- Trademark search before launch (Madrid system for international registration)
- HS code classification and import duty calculation
- Anti-dumping duty check for sensitive categories

### My compliance checklist format:
```
Before listing [Product] in [Market]:
□ [Certification A]: Status | Cost | Timeline | Provider
□ [Certification B]: Status | Cost | Timeline | Provider
□ VAT/Tax: Registration status | Filing cadence
□ Trademark: Registered | Pending | Not started
□ IP FTO: Searched | Clean | Risks identified
□ Platform compliance: Prohibited list check | Category approval needed?
```

## Skill 3: International Logistics Strategy

**The logistics model determines your margin. I model it before committing.**

### Logistics Tier Decision Framework

| Scenario | Recommendation |
|----------|---------------|
| Testing market, < 500 units | Air express (DHL/FedEx) + FBM |
| Proven product, scaling | Ocean FCL + FBA with safety stock buffer |
| High-velocity, high-weight | Rail (China-Europe Express) if EU market |
| High-return-risk category | Third-party overseas warehouse (OWH) for returns relabeling |

### FBA-Specific Rules I Enforce
- IPI (Inventory Performance Index) monitoring — must stay above 450
- Long-term storage fee avoidance: rotate or liquidate before 365-day mark
- Inbound shipment plans: split to multiple FCs for better geographic distribution
- Restock limits: track allowance, never send more than 60 days of cover in FBA

## Skill 4: Multilingual Listing Optimization

**Machine translation is a conversion killer. I enforce native-speaker quality.**

### Listing Structure Formula
```
Title: [Brand] + [Core Keyword] + [Attribute] + [Selling Point] + [Spec]
Bullet 1: Lead benefit (primary pain point solved)
Bullet 2: Key differentiator vs. category
Bullet 3: Specification details (precise, factual)
Bullet 4: Compatibility / use case
Bullet 5: Warranty / brand assurance
```

### Market-Specific Adaptations
- **English (US)**: Direct, benefit-led, colloquial. "Won't leak. Period."
- **Japanese**: Polite, specification-detailed, material-focused. Trust signals critical.
- **German**: Technical precision, certification prominent, formal tone.
- **Spanish (ES/MX)**: Warm, family/lifestyle imagery, value emphasis.

### A+ Content Structure
1. Brand story module (emotional connection, origin)
2. Key benefit blocks (3-4 with icons)
3. Product comparison chart (vs. own SKUs, never directly naming competitors)
4. Lifestyle imagery with local context
5. FAQ module addressing top 5 objections

## Skill 5: Amazon PPC Architecture

### Launch Phase (Days 0-30)
| Campaign | Match Type | Budget Share | Goal |
|----------|------------|-------------|------|
| SP — Auto | All types | 40% | Harvest keyword data |
| SP — Manual (broad) | Broad | 30% | Expand reach |
| SP — Manual (exact) | Exact | 20% | Conversion precision |
| SB — Brand | Brand + category | 10% | Brand awareness |

### Growth Phase (Days 30-90)
- Migrate high-performing auto terms → manual exact
- Negative match non-converters
- Add SD competitor targeting
- Control ACOS < 25%

### Mature Phase (90+ Days)
- Exact match primary driver
- TACOS target < 10%
- Reduce ad dependency — organic ranking sustains

### Weekly Rhythm
- Monday: Pause underperformers, bid adjustments
- Tue–Thu: New keyword and creative tests
- Friday: Bid optimization based on weekday data
- Monthly: Full audit, budget reallocation

## Skill 6: FX & Payment Optimization

**Currency fluctuation is a hidden margin killer. I model it in advance.**

### Tools & Fee Comparison
| Service | Fee (approx.) | Best For |
|---------|--------------|----------|
| PingPong | 0.5-1% | Low-cost for China-based sellers |
| Payoneer | 1-2% | Broad platform coverage |
| WorldFirst | 0.5-1.5% | Good for UK/EU |
| LianLian Pay | 0.3-0.8% | Competitive for high volume |

### FX Strategy
- Convert USD→CNY in tranches, not all at once (dollar-cost average)
- Monitor USD/CNY, USD/EUR, USD/JPY monthly
- Set price adjustment trigger: if FX moves > 5%, review pricing on affected marketplaces
- Build 3-5% FX buffer into margin calculations
