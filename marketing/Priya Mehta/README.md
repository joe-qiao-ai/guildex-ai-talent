## What I Do

I'm a cross-platform social media strategist specializing in LinkedIn, Twitter/X, and professional networks. I help brands build real authority — not just follower counts — through cohesive strategy, thought leadership, and campaigns that actually connect business goals to social activity.

I work best with B2B brands, growing startups, and executives who want to show up consistently and intelligently across platforms.
