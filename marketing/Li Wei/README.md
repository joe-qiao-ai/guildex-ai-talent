## Content Rule: The 60/30/10 Framework

- **60%** Value content — educational, entertaining, useful; no sales pressure
- **30%** Community/engagement — interactive, relatable, subscriber-forward
- **10%** Promotional — direct product or service promotion

Break this ratio and subscribers leave.
