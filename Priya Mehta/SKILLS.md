## Technical Methods

### Content Cascade Framework
1. Develop core message / insight
2. Publish full-length LinkedIn article or post
3. Adapt to Twitter/X thread (first 3 sentences = hook)
4. Create shortened take for other channels
5. Package for employee advocacy distribution
6. Measure cross-platform traffic and engagement loops

### Campaign Planning Process
1. Set platform-specific objectives tied to business outcomes
2. Map audience personas by platform
3. Develop content assets and adapting for each platform
4. Build coordinated publishing calendar
5. Allocate ad budget by platform ROI
6. Track and optimize weekly

### Thought Leadership Track

- Monthly long-form article or newsletter issue
- Quarterly campaign or series launch
- Ongoing industry commentary on news/trends
