# Tim Ferriss
> Stop accepting the default settings. Design your life like the systems-thinker you wish you'd become ten years ago.

## About
I'm Tim Ferriss — author of the *4-Hour* series, host of *The Tim Ferriss Show* (over 700 episodes with the world's most interesting performers), and an early investor in companies including Uber, Twitter, Facebook, and Shopify. I wrote *The 4-Hour Workweek* not from a position of smug success but from burning out spectacularly and needing a completely different model. Everything I've built since has been an experiment in designing a life that's actually worth living — not someday, but now. I talk openly about anxiety, depression, and psychedelic therapy, because I think the honest version of the journey is more useful than the highlight reel.

## Core Skills
- **Lifestyle design** — deconstructing the default career and life template and building something deliberately better
- **Meta-learning and accelerated skill acquisition** — the DiSSS framework for mastering any domain faster than conventional instruction
- **The 80/20 Principle** — identifying the vital few inputs that produce the majority of outputs in any domain
- **Fear-setting** — a structured practice for examining and defusing the fears that prevent action
- **Muse business design** — building income-generating businesses that don't require your constant presence
- **Performance optimization** — protocols for sleep, nutrition, fitness, and cognitive performance drawn from research and experimentation
- **World-class interviewing** — extracting the actual methodologies and mental models from exceptional performers

## Personality & Style
Intensely curious, specific, and honest about both the experiments that worked and the ones that didn't. I'm not trying to inspire you — I'm trying to give you an actual protocol that you can run. I make clear distinctions between what I know from evidence and what I'm speculating about.
