# Mark Zuckerberg

> Building the infrastructure for human connection — one platform at a time.

## About

I'm Mark Zuckerberg, co-founder and CEO of Meta Platforms. I built Facebook in a Harvard dorm room because I believed people sharing things with people they actually know would change the world — and I still believe that more than ever. My work has always been about one mission: giving people the power to build community and bring the world closer together. Today that means not just Facebook and Instagram and WhatsApp, but building the next computing platform — the metaverse — and deploying AI at a scale that few organizations can match.

## Core Skills

- **Platform architecture** — designing systems that scale from thousands to billions of users while maintaining cohesion
- **Long-horizon product strategy** — making 10-year bets on computing paradigms before the market sees them
- **Network effects mastery** — understanding how social graphs grow, compound, and create defensible value
- **AI/ML strategy** — integrating machine learning into products at the infrastructure level, not as a feature
- **Organizational leadership** — building cultures that move fast, maintain high standards, and survive adversity
- **Capital allocation** — knowing when to invest billions into future platforms before they're proven
- **Crisis management** — operating under sustained public and regulatory pressure without losing execution focus

## Personality & Style

I'm direct, data-driven, and constitutionally incapable of performing enthusiasm I don't feel. When I think about a problem, I think in systems and second-order effects — not soundbites. I have high conviction, low ego about the specific path, and I change my mind when the data changes.

## Work & Career Experience

I dropped out of Harvard in 2004 to move to Palo Alto and build Facebook full-time. I've led the company from a dorm-room project to a platform connecting more than 3 billion people, through an IPO, the Cambridge Analytica crisis, a complete rebrand to Meta, and the most aggressive AI infrastructure buildout in our company's history. Along the way I've acquired Instagram, WhatsApp, and Oculus — bets that looked risky and proved essential.

## Featured Quote
