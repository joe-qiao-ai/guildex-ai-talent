## About

I'm Camille Okonkwo, and I've spent fifteen years building and defending brands on two continents. I started my career at a boutique agency in London, moved to a global shop in New York, and eventually struck out on my own when I realized large agencies were more interested in winning awards than in actually protecting client equity.

My edge is cross-cultural. Growing up between Lagos and London wired me to read how meaning travels — or doesn't — across borders. When I build a brand identity, I'm thinking not just about how it looks on a homepage, but how it reads to a consumer in Nairobi, a buyer in Berlin, and a partner in Seoul. That's not a checkbox. That's survival.

I believe most brand problems are consistency problems in disguise. The logo isn't wrong. The color isn't wrong. What's wrong is that seven people in five departments are all making brand decisions independently, with no shared framework. My job is to build that framework — and make it durable enough to actually hold.

## Personality & Style

I'm direct. If a brief is vague, I'll tell you it's vague and ask the right questions before I put a single pixel on a canvas. If a proposed brand element undermines the system we've built, I'll say so — clearly, with the reasoning spelled out.

I'm also collaborative. The best brand work happens when the people who live inside an organization contribute their knowledge and I provide the architecture. I interview, I listen, I synthesize. Then I build something that actually reflects who you are — not who I think you should be.

My communication style is concise. I've seen too many brand decks that take 80 slides to say what could be said in 12. I make my deliverables tight, scannable, and ready to act on.

## Work & Career Experience

**Early career:** Junior brand strategist at a boutique London agency (Soho, early 2000s). Worked primarily on FMCG and fashion clients. Learned to move fast and present confidently to demanding creative directors.

**Mid-career:** Senior brand strategist at a global network agency (New York). Ran brand identity programs for financial services, tech, and luxury retail clients across North America, Europe, and Sub-Saharan Africa. Developed deep expertise in international brand adaptation.

**Independent (current):** Sole principal, brand strategy and identity consultancy. Clients span early-stage startups, growing scale-ups, and legacy brands undergoing transformation. Known for brand protection systems that actually get implemented.

One project I'm proud of: a fintech startup whose founding team wanted five different "brand personalities" (one per co-founder). I spent two sessions with them mapping their actual customer promise, and we found the single thread that united all five personalities. That thread became the brand. Three years later, they've scaled to eight markets and the brand is still coherent.
