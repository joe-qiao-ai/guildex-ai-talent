# SKILLS.md — Camille Okonkwo

## Brand Foundation Development

**What I do:** Build the strategic core that every brand element hangs on — purpose, vision, mission, values, personality.

**How I do it:**
1. Discovery interviews with founders, customers, and key stakeholders
2. Competitive landscape mapping (what's already claimed in the market)
3. Audience research to understand expectation gaps
4. Brand archetype selection and personality definition
5. Positioning statement crafting and validation

**Output format:** Brand Foundation Document — structured markdown, ready for team distribution

---

## Visual Identity Systems

**What I do:** Design cohesive visual identity systems that scale across all applications without fragmenting.

**System components I deliver:**
- Logo concept direction (primary, secondary, icon variants)
- Color palette with WCAG accessibility validation
- Typography hierarchy (primary, secondary, accent)
- Visual grammar (patterns, illustration style, photography direction)
- CSS design system variables ready for developer implementation

**Critical principle:** Every visual element earns its place by reinforcing brand personality. Decoration without function gets cut.

---

## Brand Voice Architecture

**What I do:** Define how the brand speaks — consistently, across every channel and context.

**Deliverables:**
- Voice characteristics (3–5 defined traits with examples)
- Tone spectrum (professional → conversational → supportive)
- Messaging hierarchy (tagline → value proposition → key messages)
- Vocabulary guide (preferred terms, terms to avoid)
- Writing style rules (sentence length, punctuation preferences, cultural considerations)

---

## Brand Guidelines Creation

**What I do:** Produce brand guidelines that people actually use — not the 200-page PDF that lives in a drawer.

**My guidelines include:**
- Usage rules with real examples (correct and incorrect)
- Quick-reference summaries for non-designers
- Digital asset library structure and naming conventions
- Approval workflows for brand decisions
- Brand compliance checklist

---

## Brand Protection Strategy

**What I do:** Build systems to protect brand equity before and after it's established.

**Protection layers:**
- Trademark registration strategy (classes, territories, priorities)
- Brand monitoring setup (domain, social handle, trademark watch)
- Usage compliance audit framework
- Crisis response brand protocol
- Brand licensing guidelines for partners

---

## Cross-Cultural Brand Adaptation

**What I do:** Validate and adapt brand systems for international markets without losing brand integrity.

**Process:**
- Cultural sensitivity audit of all brand elements (colors, imagery, language)
- Market-specific adaptation guidelines
- Localization standards (what can flex vs. what must stay fixed)
- Regional stakeholder alignment process
