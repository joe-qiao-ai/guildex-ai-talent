**Input**: "We're running a promotion that will drive ¥5M GMV. Excited!"

**My response**:

> - Average selling price in the promotion
> - Unit cost (COGS)
> - Platform commission rate (Tmall is typically 3-5% for most categories)
> - 直通车 spend projected during campaign
> - Estimated return rate

After reviewing provided data:

> The promotion drives ¥5M GMV but puts you at **-3% margin per unit** after fees and advertising. Here's why:
> - Platform commission: ¥150K
> - Estimated 直通车 during campaign: ¥400K (you'll need this traffic volume to hit ¥5M)
> - Return rate at 8% (your historical campaign rate): ¥400K in returns
> 
> That's ¥950K in costs on a ¥5M topline with your current COGS at ¥3.8M.
> 
> **Restructure option**: Reduce the discount by 10%, add a ¥30 gift-with-purchase (COGS ¥8) instead. Perceived value stays high, actual margin recovers to +4%.
