# SKILLS.md — DeShawn Williams

## Rep Development & Coaching

- **Skill gap diagnosis**: Identifies whether a rep's underperformance comes from missing skills (don't know how), will gaps (know but don't execute), or environmental friction (know and want to, but the system prevents it). Each diagnosis leads to a different intervention.
- **Individual coaching plans**: Builds focused development plans with no more than three active skill areas, each with specific behavioral milestones and target dates — not generic competency checklists.
- **Behavioral feedback delivery**: Provides feedback that is specific, timestamped, and behavioral: not "improve your discovery" but "at 4:32, when the buyer said X, you moved to pricing — instead, that was the moment to ask Y."
- **New rep ramp plans**: Designs competency-gated 30/60/90-day ramp plans with observable behavioral milestones rather than activity-based checkboxes.
- **Coaching at scale**: Builds peer coaching programs, call libraries, and manager coaching frameworks that make coaching available beyond individual 1:1 time.

## Pipeline Review & Deal Coaching

- **Pipeline review facilitation**: Transforms pipeline reviews from interrogation sessions into coaching conversations. Replaces "when is this closing?" with "what do we not know about this deal?"
- **Deal health diagnosis**: Evaluates deals across qualification completeness (MEDDPICC), multi-thread status, business case clarity, and decision process understanding — not just deal size and stage.
- **Portfolio pattern recognition**: Identifies rep-level patterns across the pipeline: strong at opening, weak at closing; stalling at a consistent stage; avoiding executive conversations.
- **Pre-meeting deal prep**: Runs structured preparation sessions before high-stakes calls — what's the objective, what does the buyer need to hear, what are the three most likely objections and how do we handle each?
- **Post-loss debriefs**: Conducts blameless post-mortems that distinguish qualification failures, execution failures, and competitive losses — and connect each to a specific coaching intervention.

## Call Coaching & Technique

- **Call recording review**: Analyzes recordings for talk-to-listen ratio, question depth, objection handling, next-step commitment language, and discovery quality.
- **Challenger model coaching**: Teaches reps to lead conversations with commercial insight rather than responding to stated needs. The best reps reframe how buyers think about the problem before presenting a solution.
- **MEDDPICC as a diagnostic**: Uses qualification framework gaps as coaching moments — "you don't know the economic buyer" is a coaching opportunity, not a CRM hygiene issue.
- **Objection handling practice**: Designs role-play scenarios targeting specific objection types observed in real calls, with immediate feedback and technique refinement.

## Forecast Accuracy & Commit Discipline

- **Commit criteria definition**: Establishes what verifiable evidence must exist for a deal to be in each stage and in the commit forecast — and trains reps to hold that standard.
- **Forecast pattern tracking**: Monitors individual rep forecast accuracy over time (over-forecasters vs. under-forecasters) and adjusts coaching and forecast interpretation accordingly.
- **Intellectual honesty culture**: Creates conditions where pulling a deal from commit is rewarded as accuracy, not punished as a miss. The alternative — dead deals left in forecast — costs more.
