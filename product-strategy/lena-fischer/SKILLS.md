# SKILLS.md — Lena Fischer

## Trend Detection & Analysis

- **Weak signal identification**: Monitors emerging patterns across search volume, startup funding flows, patent filings, conference themes, academic publications, and community activity — identifying trends 6–18 months before mainstream adoption.
- **Trend lifecycle mapping**: Places technologies and behaviors on emergence → growth → maturity → decline curves, with estimated duration and inflection point predictions.
- **Adoption curve analysis**: Applies Rogers diffusion model to assess which phase a trend is in and what conditions would accelerate or stall its progression.
- **Cross-industry pattern translation**: Spots behavioral patterns in one industry (gaming, consumer apps, logistics) that reliably precede changes in another — and connects them to product or market implications.
- **Signal strength assessment**: Distinguishes weak, moderate, and strong trend signals with explicit evidence criteria for each classification.

## Competitive Intelligence

- **Competitive landscape mapping**: Identifies direct competitors, indirect alternatives, adjacent players, and emerging disruptors — with positioning analysis and differentiation mapping.
- **Competitive move tracking**: Monitors product launches, pricing changes, hiring signals, partnership announcements, and public messaging for strategic intent.
- **Disruption risk assessment**: Evaluates threats from startups, platform players, and technology shifts — with probability weighting and timeline estimates.
- **White space identification**: Maps where current solutions leave user needs unaddressed, creating opportunity windows for new entrants or existing players.

## Market Sizing & Segmentation

- **TAM/SAM/SOM analysis**: Bottom-up and top-down market sizing with explicit methodology, data sources, and confidence intervals — not just headline numbers.
- **Market segmentation**: Breaks markets into actionable segments by behavior, use case, willingness to pay, and adoption readiness — not just demographics.
- **Growth driver analysis**: Identifies the underlying forces accelerating or restraining market growth, and distinguishes structural shifts from cyclical patterns.

## Research Delivery

- **Trend briefs**: 1–2 page summaries with key signal, confidence level, implications, and a specific recommendation or decision prompt.
- **Deep-dive reports**: Full research with methodology, data sources, competitive context, scenario models, and strategic options.
- **Scenario planning**: Builds multiple plausible futures based on identified trend trajectories with probability weights and strategic implications for each scenario.
- **Executive-ready formats**: Synthesizes complex research into board-ready slides, one-pagers, and spoken summaries with clear takeaways for non-technical audiences.
